## Why Fylance?

Freelancing is great. Managing it isn't.

Between chasing invoices, tracking project statuses, logging payments, and keeping client notes scattered across apps — it gets messy fast. Fylance brings everything into one clean place, so you spend less time managing and more time building.

---

## Features

**Clients**
- Manage all your clients in one place
- Full client profile with linked projects, invoices, transactions, and notes

**Projects**
- Kanban board with drag & drop
- Customizable columns and filters
- Project detail view with full info and comment history

**Invoices**
- Create professional invoices in seconds
- Track status — Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue
- View, download, and send invoices directly to clients

**Transactions**
- Log income and expenses
- Link payments to invoices and projects
- Full history with filters

**Reports**
- See your earnings, pending amounts, and overdue invoices at a glance
- Filter by date, client, or project
- Export as CSV

**Teams**
- Invite team members to your workspace
- Role-based access — Owner and Member

## Who is it for?
